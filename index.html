The kit includes 2 salt and pepper
grinders with ceramic stones. Ceramic is the best material for the mill, because it doesn’t rust or corrode. It is an ecologically clean, high quality, and absolutely safe material for humans.

To conveniently fill the spice mill, we’ve included a comfortable silicone funnel in the kit. Due to the fact that the mill is made of glass, you can easily control how full the mills are and add spices to the pepper mill/salt grinder.

An additional functional device – a spray for lemons and limes. To get lemon or lime juice you only have to screw the spray into the fruit and spray to dress salads, or extract juice for cooking and sauces. The fruit with sprayer can be stored directly in your refrigerator and be used whenever
you need it.
